What counts as Priority?

  • P1 — Business is down (network outage, email down, ransomware event)
  • P2 — Major impact (multiple users blocked, core app degraded)
  • P3 — Single user / workaround exists
  • P4 — Minor request / cosmetic / how-to

What to include

  • Exact error message
  • Time it started
  • Device name/asset tag
  • Steps you already tried

FAQ

Common fixes and quick answers.

How do I reset my password?
Use your company’s password reset portal (if available). If you don’t have access, submit a ticket with “Access Request” and include your username.
My Wi-Fi is connected but nothing loads.
Try toggling Wi-Fi off/on, then reboot. If it’s still down, note whether other devices work on the same network and submit an “Incident”.
VPN won’t connect.
Restart your computer, confirm your internet works, and try again. If you see a specific error code, include it in the ticket.
How fast will I get a response?
Typical response targets: P1 within 15–30 minutes, P2 within 1–2 hours, P3 same business day, P4 within 1–3 business days.
